About Us

Meet the baker

Jennifer Johnson

Baker and Owner

Jennifer Johnson started her new baking career out of her home in 2018. She began catering to friends and family and soon when on to participating in craft sales and community events. Through word of mouth, her business, the Bun Boutique Bakery, became more popular and is now catering for events such as birthdays and weddings.
